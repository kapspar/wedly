# Wedly Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Product Overview

Wedly is a wedding planning and vendor marketplace platform.

Initial launch focuses on Tamil weddings in the Greater Toronto Area.

Future expansion will support all wedding traditions and cultures.

---

## User Types

### Couples

Plan weddings.

### Vendors

Advertise services and manage inquiries.

### Administrators

Manage platform content and vendor listings.

---

## Auspicious Date Service

Future Paid Feature

User enters:

* Bride Birth Information
* Groom Birth Information

* Recommended Dates
* Recommended Times

Generate PDF certificate.

Priest reviews and signs.
